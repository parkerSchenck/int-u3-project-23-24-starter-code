The Journey to Treasure Island

As the captain of your crew of pirates, you must guide your crew to Treasure Island, but a storm brews in your way.

The storm was too strong, and unfortunately your ship has sunk. You never reached Treasure Island.

Patience is key and you survive the storm, but another crew has reached Treasure Island.

At the cost of the other crew, you successfully battled for the treasure. Congratulations! You are the richest pirate crew in the sea!

Sharing is caring, but in the world of piracy it doesn't matter. The other crew did not appreciate you intruding on their treasure and sunk your ship.
